Privacy Policy

Minimal and direct. This page covers what Northbase stores and why.

Northbase allows users to create accounts and store notes and files in the app. This data is stored using Supabase in order to provide syncing and core app functionality.

Northbase does not sell personal data. Data is used only to operate the product and support core features.

If you create an account, we may store information such as your email address and the files/notes you create in the app.

Users can delete files from Settings in the app, and doing so permanently deletes all files.

If you have questions about privacy, contact: northbase.app@gmail.com
